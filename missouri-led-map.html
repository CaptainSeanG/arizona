Enter your home airport any time. We'll save it and use it for the windsock card.
HOW TO USE THE MAP
  • Double tap an airport LED to make it your home airport.
  • Tap the background to toggle colors.
  • On the WINDSOCK button, tap the left or right half of the button to show on the left or right.
  • Tap the windsock card to reopen the home-airport selector.
  • Press S for the status panel.
  • Press F for fullscreen and K for kiosk cursor mode.
  • Hover over an airport LED to see the decoded weather and raw METAR.
